About me

Research interest

My research interests include many-body quantum theory and algebraic quantum fields theory.

I aim for a mathematically rigorous treatment of problems arising in these research areas, this implies dealing mainly with functional analysis, C*-algebra and their representations, as well as spectral theory.

More specifically, my current project is the study of differential equations on spaces of operators and applying them to many-body quantum problems.
